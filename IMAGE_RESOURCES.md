# Free Image Resources for Commercial Use

Resources for finding open source images to use in this portfolio project.

## Photo Sites

- **[Unsplash](https://unsplash.com/)** - High-quality photos, free for commercial use, no attribution required
- **[Pixabay](https://pixabay.com/)** - Royalty-free images, videos, vectors, and illustrations
- **[Burst by Shopify](https://www.shopify.com/stock-photos)** - Free stock photos, no attribution required
- **[FreeImages](https://www.freeimages.com)** - Free photos for personal and commercial projects

## Illustrations

- **[unDraw](https://undraw.co/)** - Minimalistic SVG illustrations with customizable colors - excellent for tech projects
- **[Freepik](https://www.freepik.com/)** - Vectors, photos, and PSD files (check license per image)

## Search Terms for Resume Manager Project

- "resume" or "CV"
- "document" or "paperwork"
- "job application"
- "career" or "professional"
- "office" or "workspace"

## Notes

- Most of these sites allow commercial use without attribution, but always verify the license for specific images
- Project images for this portfolio should be 1280x750 pixels
- Place images in the `public/` directory
